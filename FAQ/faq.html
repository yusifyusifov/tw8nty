FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

Answers to fuel your journey.

TW8NTY is founded on resonance, ambition, authenticity, legacy, boldness, innovation, unity, and vision. Each piece is designed as a ritual of identity, carrying meaning beyond fabric.

Collections are released in curated cycles — each drop represents a chapter in the TW8NTY mythology, echoing the brand’s evolving story.

We do not operate a traditional return policy. Condition and size fitting are checked at the time of delivery, ensuring every piece meets expectations before completion.

Currently, TW8NTY does not ship internationally. Deliveries are limited to local regions to ensure quality and reliability.

For now, support is available exclusively through Instagram at @the.tw8nty. Reach out via DM for assistance with orders or brand inquiries.
